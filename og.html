Founded to represent Engineers at Berkeley


Our mission is to represent the engineering student body; support engineering societies in reaching their full potential; and promote a diverse community through successful collaboration between students, faculty, societies, and the College of Engineering.

Next

Founded in 1974 as the Engineer's Joint Council

Our mission still reflects the original purpose of the EJC

Since the Engineer's Joint Council founding, we have continually supported the engineering student body at Berkeley through advocating for students' rights within the Collge of Engineering, supporting over 85 engineering student organization, and hosting events to bring together the engineering community.

For over 29 years, ESC is responsible for handling and dispersing ASUC funds to engineering student organizations. We also host one of the largest organization spaces on campus as well as one of the largest engineering themed events on campus, Engineer's Week.

Organization Support
