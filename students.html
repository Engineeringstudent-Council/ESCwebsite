Founded to represent Engineers at Berkeley


Our mission is to represent the engineering student body; support engineering societies in reaching their full potential; and promote a diverse community through successful collaboration between students, faculty, societies, and the College of Engineering.

Next

Founded in 1974 as the Engineer's Joint Council

Our mission still reflects the original purpose of the EJC

Since the Engineer's Joint Council founding, we have continually supported the engineering student body at Berkeley through advocating for students' rights within the Collge of Engineering, supporting over 85 engineering student organization, and hosting events to bring together the engineering community.

For over 29 years, ESC is responsible for handling and dispersing ASUC funds to engineering student organizations. We also host one of the largest organization spaces on campus as well as one of the largest engineering themed events on campus, Engineer's Week.

Resources We Provide

All talk and no action would be bad for us so our site has a multitude of resources to help students!

Free Menstrual Product Initiative

Our focus on creating an equitable environment for our fellow engineers thrive led to the creation of the Free Menstrual Product Initiative.

Disability and Compliance

While not a resource the ESC directly maintains, UC Berkeley has a decent resource to help those who move through life differently.

HEAR Tool

The Healthy Engineering Assistance Resource Tool is useful for both students and organizations for handling life's bumps. You are not alone! This tool has resources on mental health, therapy, etc.

Food Advice

We strive to create a strong engineering community. We provide a weekly newsletter to keep Engineering students informed about upcoming events from our affiliates and from the College of Engineering.

Housing

Like most popular locations in the world, the Bay Area has a ton a perks but housing is not one of them. Inspired to help their fellow engineers, the ESC created a guide to simplify apartment hunting.

Finance Advice

Get smart financially to get off the right footing when you graduate. Our compliation of resources should make easier to manage your finances while in college.

Academics

We provide a list of tools supported by the Engineering Student Services and the College of Engineering

Get the full Berkeley experience?

DeCals are classes taught by undergraduates and/or graduates on subjects typically not found in a university setting. Due to their popularity, the ESC compiled a list of Engineering related DeCals for engineers to check out.

The Tinker in All of Us

Berkeley is host to a multitude of makerspaces. With help from Jacobs Institute for Design Innovation, we have created an easy guide to get involved in the Maker community

The Next Unicorn Startup

Some of the greatest companies in the world have roots in Berkeley. No wonder there is a plethora of resources to create the next Apple or Intel!

Organization Support

With funding from the ASUC and support from the ESS, we help fund any ESC-affiliated organization through our Initial Allocations Applications and our FiComm Meetings.

Got a Question?

College can be confusing so we created a list of commonly asked questions that engineering students might have on a wide range of topics

Tight Knit Community

We strive to create a strong engineering community. We provide a weekly newsletter to keep Engineering students informed about upcoming events from our affiliates and from the College of Engineering.
