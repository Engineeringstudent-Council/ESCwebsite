HEAR Tool Guide


What is the HEAR Tool?

The HEAR Tool is a fully interactable table filled with filterable resources. You can filter or search columns to help find the resource you need. You can also show or hide columns.

How do I filter a column?

To filter a column, and thus in effect only find resources that match your desired needs, use the search bar or drop down menu at the top of each column. For example, if you type "Berkeley" for the column "Resource Name" you will only get resources with "Berkeley" in their name.

How do I hide/show columns?

On the bottom right of the table is text stating "Columns" with a small down arrow next to it. Click this text and a menu will appear. All the checked boxes represent which columns are hidden. To hide a column simply check the box. To show a column just uncheck the box.
