Engineering Student Council Committees

2020 - 2021

External Vice President (EVP) Committee

The External Vice President's Committee focuses on projects that involve affiliates of the College of Engineering, including clubs, administrative groups, and external organizations. Projects include but are not limited to FMPI (the Free Menstual Product Initiative), the Blue and Gold Certification Program, and the expansion of ethics education to both faculty and students.

Finance Committee (FiComm)

The Finance Committee (FiComm) is responsible for the disbursement of funds for Blue and Gold organizations. This is handled through the annual Initial Allocations process as well as year-round FiComm meetings, which give organizations a chance to request additional funds. In addition, FiComm strives to achieve a greater sense of community within the College of Engineering by organizing finance workshops and other related events.

Internal Vice President (IVP) Committee

The Internal Vice President’s Committee is centered around advocating for and supporting student organizations and the student body. In addition, the committee works to improve relationships between departments and students, focusing especially on initiatives related to equity, diversity, and inclusion.

Communications Committee

The Communications Committee is responsible for handling social media engagement and for maintaining relations with the ESS, the College of Engineering, and all Blue and Gold certified clubs. In addition, the committee is responsible for photography, videography and graphic designing for all events. Specific projects include:

  • Sending out weekly newsletters to the engineering student body
  • Managing the Universal Google Calendar
  • Organizing “Day in a life of a Berkeley Engineer” and other Instagram series
  • Recruiting new club members

    • Engineers Week Committee
