Organizations

The Engineering Student Council at Berkeley has over 60 affiliated student organizations, including competition teams, honor societies, and community outreach groups. All of these clubs have enjoyed incredible success in their fields, and they have each committed to promoting an environment of diversity and inclusion both in their club and in the broader engineering community.

Process

Beginning Spring 2020, ESC launched a new affiliation process designed to promote a healthier club culture at Berkeley. Among other things, we required our affiliated organizations to remove exclusive practices from their application processes and send their execs to a number of required trainings. We are extremely grateful to the clubs who have taken the time to complete our affiliation process and dedicate themselves to creating a better environment for all Berkeley engineers.

Finance

ESC awards funding to our affiliated organizations through Initial Allocations (spring) and Ficomm funding (periodically throughout the year). We generally have around $70,000 to award annually, so we encourage all of our affiliated clubs to apply!
