Get ready for a fun, exciting 2021 Engineers Week! Please email us at eweek@esc.berkeley.edu if you and/or your organization is interested in partnering with us.

What is Engineers Week?

Engineers Week is a weeklong event comprising of various professional, social, and design/build activities. Though primarily catered towards the 3,600+ undergraduate engineers on campus, each year Engineers Week reaches over 8,000 students from a variety of majors and degree programs and our reach is. continuing to grow. This year's event will be from Feb 8-12, 2021, and will be a hybrid event with mostly online activities. This year's theme is Berkeley Engineering: From Magic toReality. Students will learn about what happens behind those engineering magics and get a chance to connect with peers and the rest of the campus.
