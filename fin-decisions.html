Finance Decisions: FiComm Decisions & Inital Allocations

ESC awards funding to Blue and Gold certified organizations through Initial Allocations (spring) and Ficomm funding (periodically throughout the year). We generally have around $70,000 to award annually, so we encourage all certified organizations to apply!

If you are a certified organization looking to apply for funding, please consult the Funding page on the Blue and Gold portal here. If you have any questions concerning your club's finances, please contact treasurer@esc.berkeley.edu.
