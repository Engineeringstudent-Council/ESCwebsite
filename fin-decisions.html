Finance Decisions: FiComm Decisions & Inital Allocations

ESC awards funding to our affiliated organizations through Initial Allocations (spring) and Ficomm funding (periodically throughout the year). We generally have around $70,000 to award annually, so we encourage all of our affiliated clubs to apply!

If you are an affiliated organization looking to apply for funding, please consult the finance page on our affiliation portal here. If you have any questions concerning your clubs finances, please contact treasurer@esc.berkeley.edu.
