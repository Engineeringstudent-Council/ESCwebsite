Typical Events we host

  • Engineers Week

    Engineers Week is a weeklong event comprising of various professional, social, and design/build activities.

    More about EWeek

  • General Meetings

    General Meetings are where ESC discusses and presents its projects/findings/pertinent information to the engineering student body.

    Check out our Facebook events

  • Townhalls

    Sometimes part of the General Meetings, Townhalls are meant to bring to light the issues and problems that engineering students face. While the goal of townhalls differ from organization to organization, ESC's townhalls are the basis of what we do next for the following the semester.
